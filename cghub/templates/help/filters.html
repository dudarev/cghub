Help: Overview

Left side filters bar.



The left side filter bar contains six categories to choose from that can optimized your search depending on what you are looking for.


Available options:

  • By Center:
    This is the submitting center, which uploaded the data to CGHub.
  • By Sample Type:
    This is the type of sample that the DNA was derived from. (E.G. Blood, Tumor etc.)
  • By Time Modified:
    Reflects the date this data was uploaded to CGHub.
  • By Disease:
    Esophageal carcinoma, Brain Lower Grade Giloma, etc
  • By Experiment:
    RNA, DNA, WGA, etc.
  • By Library Type:
    WGS, WXS, MRE-Seq etc.
