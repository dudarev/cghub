Help: Application Flow

Start:

Navigating to the CGHub Data Browser (https://browser.cghub.ucsc.edu/browser/).

1:

Select any number of filters, from the left side Filters dropdown menus, clicking on "Apply filters" when done to invoke a query. In addition to filter selection, you can type a query string into the Search box on the menu bar as well. For more details on the Search box please see "Text Search" on the Help: Overview page. Note: The search box can be very constraining when used in conjunction with the filters options, so applying both the filters, and the search box, may yield little to no results.

2:

Select the "check box" to the left of individual results, or select the "check box" to the left of the column headers to select all results displayed on the current page. Click the "Add to cart" button to add these items to your cart, or click "Add all to cart" to move all returned results from your query to your cart. Note: Clicking "Add all to cart" may take a bit of time if you choose to add a large number of items to your cart.

3:

You may find it necessary, once you have added items to your cart, to apply new filters and add additional items to your cart. To add more items to your cart simply click on the "Browser" link (in the top menu bar) to return to the main browser view. From the main browser view, apply new filters to invoke a new search, and gather additional items for your cart. Note: Items already in your cart will stay in your cart until you remove them.

4:

You have the option to examine the metadata of the items in your cart before creating manifest.xml file.

5:

You have two options for examining the metadata of items in your cart. By examining the metadata.xml file, you can view all associated metadata related to the items in your cart. In addition, you may also download a TSV file ("summary.tsv") that can be imported into a spreadsheet to review the files in your cart. Note: TSV stands for tab-separated value; most spreadsheet applications will accept this format. However, MS Excel may only accept this file if you rename the extension to comma separated value (CSV).

6:

After examining the metadata, you may choose to remove unwanted items in your cart. To do so select the items to be removed and click on "Remove from cart" or remove all items from your cart at once by clicking on "Clear cart".

7:

Before compiling a manifest.xml file to pass to gtdownload check to see if you need to add any more items to your cart.

8:

Navigate to the "Cart" page and click on "Download manifest" to create the manifest.xml file that gtdownload will use to download the BAM files that go with your cart selections.

Finish:

Invoke gtdownload from the command line to download the BAMs you have selected by passing it the manifest.xml file.

The gtdownload program can be obtained from CGHub Software Download page. See the CGHub Download User's Guide for more information on using the gtdownload program."
