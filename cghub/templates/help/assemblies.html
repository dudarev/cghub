Help: Reference Assemblies

The reference assembly for a given BAM file is identified in the ANALYSIS metadata XML object. A single, symbolic identifier in the STANDARD tag short_name attribute identifies the assembly. All assemblies are based on one of the standard assemblies, initially produced by the Human Genome Project (HGP) and now produced by the Genome Reference Consortium (GRC). There are several minor variants of these assemblies used by the NCI sequencing centers. These are described below.

HGP (NCBI) build 36 based assemblies

NCBI-human-build36
- This is the human genome reference assemble build 36 produce by the Human Genome Project. This assembly does not contain a mitochondrial sequence. The assembly is available at ftp://ftp.ncbi.nih.gov/genomes/H_sapiens/ARCHIVE/BUILD.36.3/.
HG18
- This is the UCSC variant of the NCBI build 36 genome assembly. It includes the NC_001807 mitochondria sequence, which is not part of NCBI build 36. The haplotype regions from NCBI build 36 are included and chr*_random pseudo-scaffolds are created by linking the unlocalized scaffolds in an arbitrary order and orientation. The assembly is available at ftp://hgdownload.cse.ucsc.edu/goldenPath/hg18/chromosomes/.
NCBI36_BCCAGSC_variant
- This is a variant of the full NCBI36 human genome assembly (assembly accession GCF_000001405.12) used by the British Columbia Cancer Agency Genome Sciences Centre (BCCAGSC). It is a subset of the NCBI36 sequences from Ensembl Release 46. The chromosome 5 sequence differs from NC_000005.8 in NCBI36 in that bases 70946027..71169807 have been masked with Ns. The chromosome Y sequence differs from NC_000024.8 in NCBI36 in that the pseudo-autosomal regions (PARs) have been masked with Ns. The unlocalized and alternate loci scaffolds in NCBI36 were excluded from this assembly variant. The assembly is available at ftp://ftp.ncbi.nih.gov/genomes/H_sapiens/ARCHIVE/BUILD.36.3/special_requests/assembly_variants/.
NCBI36_BCM_variant
- This is a variant of the full NCBI36 human genome assembly (assembly accession GCF_000001405.12) used by Baylor College of Medicine Human Genome Sequencing Center (BCM-HGSC). The chromosome 3 sequence differs from that in NCBI36 in that all ambiguous bases were converted to Ns (the differences from NC_000003.10 are M->N at 60805574 and RR->NN at 60805803..60805804). The unlocalized and alternate loci scaffolds in NCBI36 were excluded from this assembly variant. The assembly is available at ftp://ftp.ncbi.nih.gov/genomes/H_sapiens/ARCHIVE/BUILD.36.3/special_requests/assembly_variants/.
NCBI36_WUGSC_variant
- This is a variant of the full NCBI36 human genome assembly (assembly accession GCF_000001405.12) used by Washington University Genome Sequencing Center (WUGSC). It is a subset of the NCBI36 sequences from Ensembl Release 46. The chromosome 5 sequence differs from NC_000005.8 in NCBI36 in that bases 70946027..71169807 have been masked with Ns. The chromosome Y sequence differs from NC_000024.8 in NCBI36 in that the pseudo-autosomal regions (PARs) have been masked with Ns. Two unlocalized chromosome 17 scaffolds (NT_113932 & NT_113938) also differ from those in NCBI36 in that the terminal gaps were dropped. The alternate loci scaffolds in NCBI36 were excluded from this assembly variant. The assembly is available at ftp://ftp.ncbi.nih.gov/genomes/H_sapiens/ARCHIVE/BUILD.36.3/special_requests/assembly_variants/.
HG18_Broad_variant
- This is a variant of the full NCBI36 human genome assembly (assembly accession GCF_000001405.12) used by the Broad Institute. This set of sequences is derived from the UCSC HG18 version of the assembly and includes the chr*_random pseudo-scaffolds created by linking the unlocalized scaffolds in an arbitrary order and orientation. The alternate loci scaffolds are not included in this assembly variant. The assembly is available at ftp://ftp.ncbi.nih.gov/genomes/H_sapiens/ARCHIVE/BUILD.36.3/special_requests/assembly_variants/.

GRC human build 37 based assemblies

GRCh37
- This is the human genome reference assemble GRCh37 produce by the Genome Reference Consortium. This assembly does not contain a mitochondrial sequence. The assembly is available at ftp://ftp.ncbi.nih.gov/genbank/genomes/Eukaryotes/vertebrates_mammals/Homo_sapiens/GRCh37/.
GRCh37-lite
- This is a subset of the full GRCh37 human genome assembly (assembly accession GCA_000001405.1) plus the human mitochondrial genome reference sequence (the "rCRS") from Mitomap.org. This set of sequences excludes all the alternate loci scaffolds of the full GRCh37 assembly, and has the pseudo-autosomal regions (PARs) on chromosome Y masked with Ns. This haploid representation of the genome is provided as a convenience for use in alignment pipelines that cannot handle the multiple placements expected in the PARs and in regions of the genome that are represented by the alternate loci. The assembly is available at ftp://ftp.ncbi.nih.gov/genbank/genomes/Eukaryotes/vertebrates_mammals/Homo_sapiens/GRCh37/special_requests/.
HG19
- This is the UCSC variant of the GRCh37 genome assembly. It includes the NC_001807 mitochondria sequence, which is not part of GRCh37 and differs from the mitochondria sequence in GRCh37-lite . The haplotype regions from NCBI build 36 are included and chr*_random pseudo-scaffolds are created by linking the unlocalized scaffolds in an arbitrary order and orientation. The assembly is available at ftp://hgdownload.cse.ucsc.edu/goldenPath/hg19/chromosomes/.
GRCh37-lite-+-HPV_Redux-build
- A description and distribution of this assembly variant is not available.
GRCh37_BI_Variant
- A description and distribution of this assembly variant is not available.
HG19_Broad_variant
- A description and distribution of this assembly variant is not available.
